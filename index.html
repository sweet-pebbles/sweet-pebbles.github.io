Dear friend,

main jaanta hu ki tum bhi merese bahut pyaar karti ho
toh saare bandhan tod-tadkar aajao mere paas
laga lo mujhe kas kar gale
baaki chod de tyagi par,

Yours lovingly,
tumhare dil ka chor
