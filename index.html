Goodbye

It's time to say goodbye. These are my last words to you. Nothing follows hereafter.
I hope all this isn't too much. I had to make sure that you don't get into any trouble in case your parents found the ball.

Ever since that first letter, I knew that any letter could be my last.
We were not able to know each other but, both of us have already created a permanent impact on each other's lives. Memories fade with time but I will always remember writing these letters as you would remember reading them. All this that happened seems more like a story in a book than something that would happen in real life. This incident was very rare and we would be very lucky if we could experience something like this again in our lives.

I will always remember you as a friend that I failed to make. I don't know your name and will probably never know it. But, I suppose it doesn't matter now what I call you. So, I am going to call you Sonya. It's the name of the girl from a book I am reading. The book is called "War and Peace". It's a Russian novel. The name is Russian too but like I said it doesn't matter now. I just need a name to remember you by.

So, my dear Sonya, I want you to know that there are no hard feelings on my side and I hope it's the same for you too.

Since it's time for us to part our ways, I want to clear all misunderstandings between us and bid you farewell.

This story doesn't have a happy ending but that doesn't mean that this letter cannot have one too.
So, I have something for you on the next page. A token of goodwill.

Goodbye Sonya
